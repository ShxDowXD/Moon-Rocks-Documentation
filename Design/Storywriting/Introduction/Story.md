Your rocket crash lands on a rocky, barren planet that partially resembles the Earth's moon, but with more variation in its terrain height - Sort of like rocky, stubborn hills.


<img src="https://github.com/ShxDowXD/Moon-Rocks-Documentation/blob/111be23445c977b83716e8bbf33951c52217f2ab/Design/Art/Moon%20Landscape.png" alt="Alt Text" width="800" height="500">


The ship is seemingly broken beyond repair, and you are forced to use parts of the rubble to create a makeshift camp. Stacking and intertwining metal components is exhausting, but after a while you manage to create a safe space for now.



However - since the ship crashed all the oxygen tanks have burst, leaving you with only the amount your suit has.
