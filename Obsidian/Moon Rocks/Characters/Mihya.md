## About

Mihya is an alien that was found abandoned as a very young child by an Astronaut while they were searching for resource-rich planets.

The Astronaut felt bad for her and brought her back to Earth in their ship, in fear of there being hostile entities also present on that moon. When they arrived back on Earth the government were furious that the Astronaut had brought a potentially dangerous alien back and killed them right in front of child Mihya.

Mihya grew up in a secret bunker hidden in the mountains, kept away from society in fear that she could be a danger to the rest of the world. She was assigned a carer who looked after her and taught her English, which let Mihya communicate with them and tell her story.
***

## Design

Mihya is a pink, blue and purple alien with two sharp fangs and large curly eyebrows.

She wears a dark pink jacket and a blue scarf.

Mihya's face is in the shape of a piechart, allowing her to convey emotions by rotating and changing the proportions and angle of it.
